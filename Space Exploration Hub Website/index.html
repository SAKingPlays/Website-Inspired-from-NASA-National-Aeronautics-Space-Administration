Pakistan’s first fully indigenous Earth observation satellite, launched from China’s Jiuquan Satellite Launch Centre using a Kuaizhou-1A rocket.

James Webb Telescope

Infrared telescope, observes distant galaxies, stars, exoplanets, revealing universe’s origins.

International Space Station

Orbiting laboratory, hosts multinational research, advances space exploration, human spaceflight tech.

Curiosity Rover

Mars rover, studies Martian geology, climate, assesses past habitability for microbial life.

Lunar Gateway
